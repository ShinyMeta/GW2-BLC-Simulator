# Simulates opening Black Lion Chests in Guild Wars 2

From Anet's official disclosure of odds at: help.guildwars2.com/hc/en-us/articles/26106005230611-Disclosure-of-Odds

## Disclosure of Odds in Black Lion Chests

Each Black Lion Chest guarantees four drops out of the total pool of items:

- 1x Black Lion Statuette.
- 1x Guaranteed Item, varies each time the Chest updates.
- 2x Items in the Common Category, defined as the pool of items each with a 2.9% or higher drop rate.

Additionally, there is a 10% chance to get an additional fifth item which draws exclusively from the Returning Exclusive, New Exclusive, Uncommon, Rare, and Super-Rare pools, as follows:

1. Returning Exclusive has a 1.2% drop chance from the additional fifth slot.
2. New Exclusive has a 1.9% drop chance from the additional fifth slot.
3. Uncommon items have a combined drop chance of 5%. They each have a 0.22% to 0.99% drop chance from the additional fifth slot.
4. Rare items have a combined drop chance of 1.8%. They each have a 0.06% to 0.09% drop chance from the additional fifth slot.
5. Super Rare items have a combined drop chance of 0.04%. They each have a 0.006% drop chance from the additional fifth slot.
